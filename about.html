Welcome to Mz School — a platform dedicated to teaching programming and web development in Arabic. Our goal is to make high-quality tech education accessible for everyone across the Arab world.

We believe that learning programming should be simple, free, and in your own language. Follow our lessons to build real-world coding skills step by step.
